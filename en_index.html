About

We create a Fantasy World for Children around the World.

At Wecref, we create a fantasy world where children can dream, imagine, and explore freely.

Our mission is to provide safe, meaningful, and engaging content for children.

Through our lovable characters and creative stories, we bring joy and inspiration to children worldwide.

We strive to create content that families can enjoy anytime, anywhere.

Our goal is to deliver magical experiences filled with wonder and imagination.

Chaipang Kids

As Wecref's flagship brand, Chaipang Kids offers children's content

where young learners can play, sing, and explore the Chaipang Fantasy World

with the Chaipang Friends characters while learning naturally.

We create app-based educational content for children

through YouTube videos, animations, books, and educational materials.

We also operate a global character IP business,

bringing our original characters to children around the world.
