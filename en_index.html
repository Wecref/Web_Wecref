About us

We create a Fantasy World for Children around the World.

We aim to create a fantasy world for children around the world, both online and offline,
by creating and providing 'highly informative, safe, and exciting' content.

We are expanding our business areas, centering on brands
such as Chaipang Kids and Wecref Games brands.

Chaipang Kids

Chaipang Kids is a children's educational content
that children from all over the world can learn on their own
while playing, singing, and having fun with Chaipang friends
in the Fantasy world of Chaipang.

With Chaipang Chinese as our leading service,
we are expanding our educational contents such as
Chaipang addition, subtraction, and multiplication.
