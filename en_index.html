About us

We create a Fantasy World for Children around the World.

Wecref aims to create a fantasy world for children around the world,
both online and offline, by offering highly informative, safe, and exciting content.

Wecref is expanding its business scope, focusing on brands
like Chaipang Kids and Wecref Games.

Chaipang Kids

Chaipang Kids is a children's educational content
that children from all over the world can learn on their own
while playing, singing, and having fun with Chaipang friends
in the Fantasy world of Chaipang.

Chaipang Kids is expanding
its educational content to include foreign languages,
math, art play, and safety education.
